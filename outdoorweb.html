Outdoor Recreation

Explore the Outdoors

  • Historic Walking Tour Culver City's downtown area stands as one of the most walkable cities in America, with pedestrian-friendly streets, diverse shops, and vibrant cultural attractions.
  • Culver City Stairs & Overlook The Culver City stairs and scenic Baldwin Hills overlook provide both a challenging workout and breathtaking views, offering fitness enthusiasts a rewarding ascent while showcasing stunning panoramas of the cityscape and surrounding landscapes.
  • Bike Path to the Beach You can enjoy a scenic bike ride from Culver City to the beach by following the Ballona Creek bike path, offering a refreshing journey through nature and urban landscapes.
  • Farmers Market Visiting Culver City's farmers market offers a delightful opportunity to explore local produce, artisanal goods, and vibrant community atmosphere.
  • Kenneth Hahn Kenneth Hahn State Recreation Area, renowned for its picturesque landscapes, includes the tranquil beauty of the duck pond, providing visitors with a serene escape into nature.
  • Take a Plunge This Culver City Pool, located at Veteran's Park, offers a refreshing aquatic experience for residents and visitors seeking leisure and fitness activities.
  • Parks Culver City oversees 13 parks, a regional Senior Center, Municipal Plunge, Dog Park, Skate Park, Tennis Courts, Handball Courts, Basketball Courts, Playgrounds and more!

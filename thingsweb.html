Things to Do in Culver City

Local Favorites of Culver

  • The Museum of Jurassic Technology offers a captivating blend of art, science, and history, challenging traditional norms with its unconventional exhibits. Established in 1988, it presents an array of oddities and curiosities that blur the lines between fact and fiction, inviting visitors to question perceptions of reality and explore the boundaries of human knowledge.
  • Sony Pictures Studio Tour offers a captivating glimpse into the world of filmmaking at one of Hollywood's iconic studios. Founded in 1918, Sony Pictures Studios has been a major player in the entertainment industry, producing countless beloved films and television shows. The tour provides visitors with behind-the-scenes access to soundstages, sets, and props, immersing them in the magic of movie-making. With a rich history spanning over a century, Sony Pictures Studios continues to be a hub for creativity and innovation in the world of entertainment.
  • Kirk Douglas Theatre is a modern venue known for its diverse theatrical productions. Named after the legendary actor Kirk Douglas, it serves as a platform for emerging and established artists. With its intimate setting and commitment to artistic excellence, the theatre enriches the cultural landscape of Los Angeles.
  • STAR Eco Station is an educational facility focused on environmental conservation and wildlife preservation. Founded in 1997, it provides interactive exhibits and hands-on programs for visitors of all ages to learn about sustainability and biodiversity. With its commitment to environmental stewardship, the STAR Eco Station serves as a vital resource for raising awareness and inspiring action to protect our planet.
